SLAM-seq defines direct gene-regulatory functions of the BRD4-MYC axis

Transcription Factors

Transcription Factors (and Chromatin Regulators):

  • When normally regulated:
    • govern the identity and fate of a cell
  • when mutated or dysregulated:
    • drives cancer and other diseases

Epigenetic Regulators

Epigenetic means:

  • heritable change in gene function that does not involve changes in DNA sequence
  • where epi means "in addition to" in Greek
  • and the effects are most often changes in the chromosome
  • but would include prions too
  • the changes must be inheritable, either in the progeny of the cells or organism

Types of Epigenetic Regulators

  • Regulators that support normal cell function
  • Regulators that somehow maintain aberrant states
    • !!!!Accessible Entry Points for Targeted Therapy!!!!

Therapy Targeted on "Aberrant" Epigenetic Regulators
Example:BET bromodomain inhibitors (a.k.a. BETis )

  • Theraputic activity in leukemia (and other cancer) models
  • ... but with poorly understood mechanisms

Vi-Compatability "Switched Off"

Vim manuals often assume you are using Vim with the Vi-compatibility switched "off". To check the Vi-compatibility status, use:
:set compatibility?

If it responds with "nocompatible" it is set correctly. Apparently thre new .vimrc set it correctly. I really should look to see what I put into my vim set up!

Today I Learned about Diabetic Retinopathy

April 22, 2018

Diabetic Retinopathy

  • is a common complication of diabetes
  • the leading cause of blindness in U.S. adults
  • can be detected by analysing a patient's retina

Mechanism of Diabetic Retinopathy

  • in the eye, tiny blood vessels leak, making the retina swell
  • when the macula swells, it is called macular edema
    • "The macula is the central area of the retina and is of particular interest to retina specialists. Remember, that the retina is the light sensitive tissue which lines the inside of the eye. The macula is the functional center of the retina. It gives us the ability to see “20/20” and provides the best color vision." VMR Institute
    • Edema is swelling caused by excess fluid trapped in your body's tissues.
  • This is the most common reason why people with diabetes lose their vision

Exciting New Retinopathy Screening System

  • Artifical Intelligence based disease screening software
  • first AI screening software approved by FDA
  • requires a special retinal camera
  • Accuracy
    • 87.4% of patients with the disease
    • 89.5% of patients without the disease
    • positives then referred to eye specialist

The Cool Thing

"If widely adopted, the software could make it easier for more patients to get regularly screened for the condition"

From Science Vol:360;6386,pg 244(April 20, 2018)
