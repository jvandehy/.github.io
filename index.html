Today I Learned about Diabetic Retinopathy

April 22, 2018

Diabetic Retinopathy

  • is a common complication of diabetes
  • the leading cause of blindness in U.S. adults
  • can be detected by analysing a patient's retina

Mechanism of Diabetic Retinopathy

  • in the eye, tiny blood vessels leak, making the retina swell
  • when the macula swells, it is called macular edema
    • "The macula is the central area of the retina and is of particular interest to retina specialists. Remember, that the retina is the light sensitive tissue which lines the inside of the eye. The macula is the functional center of the retina. It gives us the ability to see “20/20” and provides the best color vision." VMR Institute
    • Edema is swelling caused by excess fluid trapped in your body's tissues.
  • This is the most common reason why people with diabetes lose their vision

Exciting New Retinopathy Screening System

  • Artifical Intelligence based disease screening software
  • first AI screening software approved by FDA
  • requires a special retinal camera
  • Accuracy
    • 87.4% of patients with the disease
    • 89.5% of patients without the disease
    • positives then referred to eye specialist

The Cool Thing

"If widely adopted, the software could make it easier for more patients to get regularly screened for the condition"

From Science Vol:360;6386,pg 244(April 20, 2018)
